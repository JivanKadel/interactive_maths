Sets: What is the cardinality of the set A = {2,4,6,8,10}\{2, 4, 6, 8, 10\}{2,4,6,8,10}?

Options:

  • A) 4
  • B) 5
  • C) 6
  • D) 10
Details

Reveal Solution The set has 5 elements → Answer: B) 5

Triangle: In triangle ABC, if angle A = 60°, angle B = 70°, find angle C.

Options:

  • A) 50°
  • B) 60°
  • C) 70°
  • D) 80°
Details

Reveal Solution Sum of angles = 180° → C = 180° - (60° + 70°) = 50° → Answer: A) 50°

Trigonometry: If sin θ = 3/5, and θ is acute, what is cos θ?

Options:

  • A) 4/5
  • B) 5/3
  • C) 1/2
  • D) 2/5
Details

Reveal Solution Use identity: sin²θ + cos²θ = 1
→ cos²θ = 1 - (9/25) = 16/25 → cos θ = 4/5 → Answer: A) 4/5

Factorization: Factorize: x² + 7x + 12

Options:

  • A) (x + 3)(x + 4)
  • B) (x + 6)(x + 2)
  • C) (x + 1)(x + 12)
  • D) (x + 2)(x + 5)
Details

Reveal Solution Middle term split: 3 + 4 = 7, 3×4 = 12 → Answer: A) (x + 3)(x + 4)

HCF & LCM: Find the LCM of 12 and 15.

Options:

  • A) 60
  • B) 180
  • C) 30
  • D) 45
Details

Reveal Solution Prime factors:
12 = 2² × 3
15 = 3 × 5
LCM = 2² × 3 × 5 = 60 → Answer: A) 60

Statistics: Find the mean of: 5, 10, 15, 20, 25

Options:

  • A) 15
  • B) 10
  • C) 20
  • D) 12
Details

Reveal Solution Mean = (5 + 10 + 15 + 20 + 25)/5 = 75/5 = 15 → Answer: A) 15

Probability: A die is rolled. What is the probability of getting an even number?

Options:

  • A) 1/6
  • B) 1/2
  • C) 2/3
  • D) 1/3
Details

Reveal Solution Even numbers: 2, 4, 6 → 3 outcomes out of 6 → Probability = 3/6 = 1/2 → Answer: B) 1/2
